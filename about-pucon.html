About Pucon

Pucon is a small mountain town located in the Araucania, one of the southern districts of Chile. The town, with little more than 13.000 inhabitants, is the preferred place in the region to spend leisure time with friends and family every season of the year. During summer (December to March), Pucon offers amazing rafting, kayaking, horseback riding, zip line rides, skydiving and all kinds of watersports in the beautiful lake Villarrica, whose most popular beach is right next to Puconâ€™s downtown. During winter (March to Jun), visitors and locals enjoy the natural hot springs, and the amazing tracks for snowboarding and ski on the Villarrica volcano apart from the engaging nightlife in town.

About Chile

Chile is well known for the incredible variety of different landscapes you can find across its regions. From the driest desert of the world in the north to the glaciers in the South. To the East, the majestic Andes Mountains run all along the border and to the west, the Pacific Ocean provides the most incredible sunsets for the many coastal cities of the country.
